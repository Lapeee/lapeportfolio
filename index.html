    Frevent app

    Frevent app Details

    App that connects you to other people. You can add other users to your friends list and chat with them. You can also create events and invite your friends to them. You can also see events that your friends have created. Companies can advertise their events and normal users can buy tickets to them. All data is stored in a database. All logins are authorized and bearer tokens are used to authenticate users. The app is made with ReactJS and NodeJS.

    Climate change app

    Safe with arduino

    Arduino Safe details

    Project made with arduino. Originally school project but modified it a little by myself. The safe has a keypad where you can enter a 4 digit code to open the safe. The safe also has a motion sensor that detects if someone is trying to open the safe. If someone tries to open the safe without the correct code, the safe will send a text message to the owner of the safe. The safe also has a led light that lights up when the safe is opened. Owned can also change the code of the safe by entering the old code and then entering the new code. Project was made with arduino and C++.

    ATM with C++

    ATM details

    This project is ATM made with C++ and Qt Creator. The ATM has a login screen where you can login with your card number and pin code. After you have logged in you can withdraw money, deposit money, transfer money to another account and check your balance. Also i made it possible for user to login to bank with physical card by using RFID reader. Database used in this project was mySQL. All logins are authorized and bearer tokens are used to authenticate users.
